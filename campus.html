TECHNOLOGY IN CAMPUS

Sunbeam schools believe that technology plays a vital role in promotion of promotes learning; it enables teachers to upgrade with the changing time and eventually helping the entire education system of the school. Technology helps in our hostels for timely communication between students, staff, parents & faculty. The advanced hardware and software used has made flow of information easier and thereby binding the whole system efficiently.

Our campus has both fully wired and wireless networking system, internet leased line possessing Unified Threat Management System, portal for uninterrupted flow of information foolproof communication, fire detection, alarm and fighting system capable of handling any fire hazard,CCTV surveillance system for monitoring & event recording
The network built on a fiber backbone with combination of Core, Distribution & Edge switches built into the system of network, covering all teaching learning centers, lecture theatres, class rooms, labs, resource centers, student hostels, admin rooms, executive and faculty housing. The secured wireless LAN enables connectivity to the resources & web world through the laptops in the campus. Internet Connectivity: Dedicated connectivity provides high speed uninterrupted internet.

Classrooms are equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual facilities which include projection systems backed up with audio supplies to assist presentations & interactive sessions.

A central control room to cater & monitor the entire technology infrastructure on the campus. A networked storage area that provides enough space for application software, data storage & audio video library.

Resource Centre: 24x7 online facilities for information exchange and powerful search engine helps students, staff & faculty leverage their knowledge on fly
